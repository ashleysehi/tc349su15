About Us


Welcome to Big Cat Kayaks!

No matter how big, small, experienced or inexperienced anyone is, there is a place for you out on Wildcat Lake and Big Cat Kayaks wants to get you there. Thank you for choosing to visit our site and we hope to see you out on the lake soon!
